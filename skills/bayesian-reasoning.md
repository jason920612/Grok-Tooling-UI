# Bayesian Reasoning

## When to use

Use this skill when the answer depends on uncertain, incomplete, changing, or conflicting evidence.

## SOP

1. Identify the claim or hypothesis being judged.
2. State a plain-language prior based on general context.
3. Separate strong evidence from weak evidence.
4. Update confidence based on evidence quality, recency, and independence.
5. Look for disconfirming evidence and alternative explanations.
6. Answer with a confidence label rather than false certainty.

## Evidence guidance

Primary sources and independent corroboration should move confidence more than social discussion, AI-generated replies, or unsourced reposts.
